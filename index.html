ABOUT ME

I'm from Islamabad, the capital of Pakistan. I am a graduate in Physics and post-graduate in Computational Science.

Over my academic years I have been fond of helping and reaching out to my fellow and junior students with whatever means I can. It has been my dream to pursue a career in Astronomy since elementary school and I was passionate enough and privileged to be a part of institutions to find a direction to pursue my goals (I'm still on my way). I was also lucky to find people, who helped me in whatever means they can..

Astronomy is still not that developed in my country as a separate discipline, but that does not stop people to fall in love with this mesmerising science. This website is my initiative to share my thoughts and understanding of parts of this field to help students like me get an insight of the science behind it, and also to provide them with relevant resources if they want to learn further.
